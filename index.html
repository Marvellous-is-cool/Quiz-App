Quiz App

Instructions
1. The quiz contains 15 questions that are based on Nigeria's constitution
2. There is a 20 seconds countdown timer for every questions which is located at the right top corner, once the timer stops, the next question appears automatically.
3. On providing answers for each questions, the next question appears automatically as well.
4. On completion of the quiz, a screen will display for your quiz score. You are expected to score atleast 50%.
5. Once you click on the start button, your timer starts counting..
